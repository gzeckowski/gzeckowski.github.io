    • M.S. GIS Advanced Programming Course - Red Fox Population in Prince George's County in Jupyter Notebook
      Click here! Red Fox Population in Prince George's County in Jupyter Notebook

      This is my final report for the Advanced Programming Course in my Master's GIS Program at UMD. I used Jupyter Notebook to gather iNaturalist, Road Centerlines, Population Census Data County Census Data, and Major Cities in Prince Georges County (PGCo), Maryland on Red Fox Populations. The report includes all of the data compiled into one map and shows where Red Foxes fall within the major cities of PGCo to 1.5 kilometers.

    • M.S. GIS Spatial Modeling Course - Cinnamon Teal Habitat Suitability Analysis

      This is my final report for the Spatial Modeling Course in my Master's GIS Program at UMD. I used ArcGIS Pro ModelBuilder to analyze factors affecting the Cinnamon Teal's Range in a habitat friendly location to a more urban location. The report includes workflow diagrams and conclusions based on model validation.

      This is my final report for the Digital Image Processing and Analysis Course in my Master's GIS Program at UMD.
